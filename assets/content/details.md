Karanwal Lime Chem I Private Limited (KLC) was established in 1999, is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality Calcium Carbonate products, specialising in Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC), and Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC).

### **Our Products**

At Karanwal Lime Chem (KLC), we offer a comprehensive range of Calcium Carbonate products designed to meet the diverse requirements of multiple industries. Our product portfolio includes:

#### **Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC)**

Our PCC is synthetically processed to achieve high purity, controlled particle size, and superior brightness. It is widely used in industries such as rubber, PVC, paper, paints, pharmaceuticals, and more. KLC's PCC grades are known for their consistency, excellent dispersion, controlled bulk density and performance enhancement properties.

#### **Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)**

KLC's GCC (also known as Natural Calcium Carbonate) is manufactured from high-quality natural calcite, with state-of-the-art ultrafine grinding mill, finely ground to deliver optimal particle size distribution and reliable performance. It is an ideal solution for applications in paper, PVC, paints, coatings, and other industrial uses where cost-effectiveness and quality are essential.

### **Applications Across Industries**

**Tailored Calcium Carbonate Solutions for Diverse Industrial Needs**

KLC's Calcium Carbonate solutions enhance product performance, process efficiency, and cost-effectiveness across a wide range of industries.

#### **Rubber Industry**

Used in tyres, tubes, conveyor belts, footwear, and molded rubber goods to improve strength, durability, and processing efficiency.

1. Improves tensile strength, stiffness, and hardness
2. Enhances abrasion resistance and product life
3. Provides better processing and surface finish
*Recommended Products:* KLC-11, KLC-111, KLC-11 Ultra, KLC-121

#### **Plastics & PVC Industry**

Widely used in PVC pipes, conduits, and polymer compounds to enhance rigidity, dimensional stability, and processing performance.

1. Improves stiffness and ring strength
2. Enhances impact resistance with fine grades
3. Improves extrusion efficiency and heat stability
*Recommended Products:* KLC-10, GCC

#### **Paper Industry**

Used in writing, printing, and coated papers to improve brightness, opacity, and print quality while reducing pulp consumption.

1. Enhances whiteness and opacity
2. Improves surface smoothness and printability
3. Enables cost-effective paper production
*Recommended Products:* GCC, KLC-121

#### **Paints & Coatings**

Used as an extender pigment to improve coating performance, finish, and cost efficiency in paints.

1. Improves dispersion and coverage
2. Controls gloss and surface finish
3. Enhances durability and weather resistance
*Recommended Products:* KLC-111, KLC-11, GCC, KLC-121

#### **Adhesives & Sealants**

Used in various adhesive systems to control flow properties, improve bonding strength, and enhance formulation stability.

1. Provides excellent rheology and slump resistance
2. Improves tensile strength and cohesion
3. Reduces shrinkage and enhances consistency
*Recommended Products:* KLC-10, GCC

#### **Detergent Industry**

Used in detergent powders, cakes, and cleaning products to improve formulation efficiency and performance.

1. Improves bulk density and flow properties
2. Acts as a filler and anti-caking agent
3. Provides mild abrasive cleaning action
*Recommended Products:* KLC-121, KLC-11

#### **Pharmaceutical Industry**

Used as both an active ingredient and excipient in tablets and healthcare formulations requiring high purity and safety.

1. Acts as an effective calcium supplement and antacid
2. Provides excellent compressibility in tablets
3. Ensures stability and controlled pH
*Recommended Products:* KLC-111, KLC-11 Ultra, KLC-101

#### **Food Industry**

Used as a food additive (E170) for fortification, stabilisation, and texture enhancement in various food products.

1. Provides calcium enrichment
2. Acts as an acidity regulator and firming agent
3. Improves texture and flow properties
*Recommended Products:* KLC-111, KLC-11 Ultra, KLC-101

#### **Cosmetics & Personal Care**

Used in powders, creams, and oral care products for texture improvement, oil absorption, and finishing quality.

1. Excellent oil absorption and mattifying effect
2. Provides smooth texture and opacity
3. Acts as a mild abrasive in oral care
*Recommended Products:* KLC-111, KLC-11 Ultra

#### **Glass Industry**

Used as a stabiliser and flux in glass manufacturing to improve durability and processing efficiency.

1. Enhances chemical durability and strength
2. Reduces melting temperature and energy consumption
3. Improves clarity and finish
*Recommended Products:* GCC, KLC-121

#### **Construction & Building Materials**

Used in cement boards, pipes, and construction materials to enhance strength, durability, and workability.

1. Improves structural strength and stability
2. Enhances workability and finish
3. Provides cost-effective formulation
*Recommended Products:* GCC

#### **Ceramics Industry**

Used in tiles, sanitary ware, and glazes to improve finish, durability, and thermal performance.

1. Acts as a flux for better melting and bonding
2. Improves whiteness and surface finish
3. Controls shrinkage and cracking
*Recommended Products:* GCC

#### **Friction Materials**

Used in brake linings and clutch facings to stabilize performance and improve durability.

1. Maintains consistent friction performance
2. Reduces noise and vibration
3. Improves heat resistance and wear life
*Recommended Products:* KLC-11, KLC-111
